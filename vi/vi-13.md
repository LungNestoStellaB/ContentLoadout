# Clean Script
You come home with a new medication.

Long name.
Tiny print.
Possible side effects listed like a legal thriller.
And now you are meant to feel calm about swallowing it.

That is not confidence.
That is compliance.

So you do what more people should be doing.
You take a photo of the label and leaflet.
You ask AI to explain what the medicine is for.
What the common side effects are.
What questions you should ask your doctor or pharmacist.

Not to replace them.
To walk in prepared.

That changes everything.
You stop nodding along.
You start understanding your own care.

For a lot of people, that is the difference between fear and control.

Just ask AI.
