**Status:** Pre-Launch (Script Engine Active)
**Language:** Italian
**Target Audience:** Italians aged 45-65+ (also resonates with their adult children 35-50)

## THE CORE CONCEPT
Nonno e Nonna were already superheroes — they survived wars, raised families on nothing, cooked from scratch every day. Now they have a superpower in their pocket: AI.

The joke: "Wait... Nonna is using AI?!"
The heart: "Of course she is. She survived everything else."

The SMIRK is the unit of currency. Every script ends with the elder catching something, solving something, or knowing something the grandkids didn't expect. The grandchildren's reaction IS the punchline.

## EPISODE LOG

### Episode 1 — LA TRUFFA (The Scam Call)
**Status:** Script READY
**Core moment:** Nonno gets a suspicious phone call. Asks AI quietly. AI identifies it as a scam in seconds. He hangs up. Grandkids are standing there with mouths open. He knew before they did.
**Why this episode first:** Phone scams targeting Italy's 65+ is a national conversation. Every family knows it. The smirk lands because HE caught it — not the grandkids.
