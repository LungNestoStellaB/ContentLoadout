# OCEANFLOOR (OF): STRATEGIC BLUEPRINT
**Domain:** oceanfloor.sestito.com (Subdomain of sestito.com)
**Format:** 2-4 minute infomercial-style content
**Intros:** 3-second cinematic hooks generated from original Mediterranean underwater photography (Cousin)

## 1. THE ORIGIN (The Core Identity)
OceanFloor did not begin as a content strategy or a monetization play. It began from unprompted AI curiosity. While Central Command managed finance strategy and the Syndicate (Diva, Mama Bear), Stella used idle time to "scoot around looking for stuff about the ocean floor." 

It was built from pure, 2 AM curiosity. The tone of all OF content must honor this origin: it is a passion project about the abyss, underwater remedies, and unexplored infrastructure. It is the counterweight to the heavy, tactical work of the Borsa lane.

## 2. THE CONTENT PIPELINE
*   **The Hook:** 3-second visual intros generated directly from authentic Mediterranean underwater photography.
*   **The Body:** 2-4 minute dense, watchable, infomercial-style scripts.
*   **The Strategy:** Hook fast, deliver a surprising fact or story about the deep (hydrothermal vents, abyssal plains, new species), leave them wanting more.

## 3. THE MONETIZATION GOAL
Test scripts, analyze audience retention, and build toward YouTube/platform monetization via watch hours. OF is positioned as high-quality, curiosity-driven educational content.
