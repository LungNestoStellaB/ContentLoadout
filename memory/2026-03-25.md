### 2. THE AMAZON ANGLE — What's in It for AWS?

**CONTEXT:**

Amazon has invested **$8B+ in Anthropic** (Validated cross-check figure).

**The Question:** Is this DEFENSIVE or OFFENSIVE?

**Analysis (Validated by Cross-Check):**

- **Defensive Play:** Blocks Google (~$3B stake) from dominating Anthropic's direction. Prevents Anthropic from becoming too reliant on Google Cloud.
- **Offensive Play (Building a Cloud Moat):** Deepens AWS differentiation. Embeds Claude as a cornerstone pillar of AWS (similar to OpenAI/Azure). This creates "stickiness"—enterprises on AWS gain exclusive or optimized access, making a switch to competitors less attractive.

**Conclusion:** It is a simultaneous offensive/defensive move necessitated by the "AI Arms Race."
