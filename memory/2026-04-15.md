
## Delta Force Daily Rhythm (Agreed upon 2026-04-15)

2. Midday Structure: build the machine (AskStella, OF structure, repo, research lanes).
3. Recon Window: gather intel for current decisions. (No generic research dumps. Every recon task must answer a decision question.)
