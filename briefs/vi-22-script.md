# VI 22 Script: The 3-Step AI Voice Scam Defense

**Format:** Short Form (TikTok, Reels, Shorts)
**Pacing:** Urgent hook, clear relief, actionable steps.
**Angle:** Making clear a big fear.

**[0:00 - 0:05] THE HOOK (Visual: You holding a phone, looking stressed, then looking directly at the camera with resolve. Background: Dark, maybe a flickering light.)**
**Audio:** "Your phone rings. It's your kid, and they're crying for help. But what if it's not actually them?"

**[0:05 - 0:15] THE FEAR (Visual: Split screen. One side: A shadowy figure typing on a laptop. Other side: A parent looking panicked. Text on screen: "AI Voice Cloning takes 3 seconds of audio.")**
**Audio:** "Scammers only need a three-second clip of your voice from social media to clone it perfectly using AI. It sounds exactly like you. It breathes like you. It panics like you."

**[0:15 - 0:25] THE RELIEF (Visual: The dark background shifts to bright, calming colors. You step into a well-lit room, looking confident. Text on screen: "The 3-Step Defense.")**
**Audio:** "It’s terrifying. But you don't need to be a tech expert to stop it dead in its tracks. Here is your family's three-step defense. Do this tonight."

**[0:25 - 0:35] STEP 1 (Visual: Text on screen: "1. The Safe Word." You hold up one finger.)**
**Audio:** "Step one. Establish a family safe word right now. Make it something completely random. If someone calls asking for money or help, ask for the word. No word? Hang up."

**[0:35 - 0:45] STEP 2 (Visual: Text on screen: "2. Hang Up & Call Back." You mime hanging up a phone and dialing.)**
**Audio:** "Step two. If you feel panicked, that's the scammer's goal. Hang up the phone immediately, and call them right back on the number *you* have saved in your contacts."

**[0:45 - 0:55] STEP 3 (Visual: Text on screen: "3. Lock Down Social Media." You tap a 'Private Account' toggle on a graphic.)**
**Audio:** "Step three. Stop giving them ammunition. Lock your social media accounts down to 'friends only' so strangers can't scrape your voice."

**[0:55 - 1:00] THE CALL TO ACTION (Visual: You pointing at the camera. Text: "Share this with family.")**
**Audio:** "This is the easiest way to beat the hackers. Share this video with your family right now to set up your safe word today."
