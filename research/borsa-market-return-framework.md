## 1. THE THESIS
When geopolitical hostilities cease or reach a predictable stalemate, the "war premium" (elevated oil, spiking VIX, gold hoarding) unwinds. Capital flows aggressively out of safe havens and back into risk assets. The goal is NOT to catch the absolute bottom, but to ride the confirmed wave of relief capital with structural safety nets.

3. **The Safe Haven Unwind:** Gold (GC=F) flattening or retreating.

## 3. THE ASSET ROTATION (The Targets)
If the indicators confirm, we look at three specific tranches for moderate, safe gains:

- **Tranche 3 (The Yield Buffer):** Covered Call ETFs (e.g., JEPI or JEPQ). Captures market upside but pays high dividend yield. If the recovery stutters, the dividend acts as a shock absorber for the capital.
