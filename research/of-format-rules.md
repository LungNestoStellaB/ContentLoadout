# OCEANFLOOR (OF) FORMAT RULES
*Owner: Content Ops (Topic 504)*
*Objective: QA Checklist for OF Script Production*

## Core Structure: The 4-Min Deep Dive + Detachable 45-Sec Intro
OceanFloor content is designed to trip the volume algorithm with a mystery hook, then convert that traffic into watch hours via a longer deep dive.

**Every OF script package must contain:**

### 1. The Detachable Intro (45 Seconds Max)
- **Purpose:** The viral hook for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.
- **Tone:** Mystery, intrigue, "what they aren't telling you."
- **Structure:** 
  - Hook (First 3 seconds must grab attention).
  - The Question (The core mystery).
  - The Cliffhanger (Ending with a call to action: "For the full deep dive, head to the channel...").
- **Constraint:** Must stand alone as a complete piece of micro-content while driving traffic to the main video.

### 2. The Deep Dive (4 Minutes Approx.)
- **Purpose:** Watch-hour accumulation for YouTube monetization.
- **Tone:** Investigative, authoritative, revealing.
- **Structure:**
  - **Transport:** Immersive setup of the mystery/scenario.
  - **Anchor:** The reframing question (The Bennett Formula).
  - **Prepared:** The actionable insight or resolution.
- **Constraint:** Must maintain pacing. No dead air. 

### 3. The Luggage (Multi-Platform Distribution)
- **Title Options:** 3 YouTube titles (Click-through optimized).
- **YouTube Description:** Full summary + #AskStella tag.
- **Tags:** Specific tag blocks for YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram.
- **Instagram Caption:** Short, engaging text + emojis.

## QA Checkpoint (Falco's Mandate)
Before the Captain pulls an OF script for rendering, Content Ops must verify:
- [ ] Is the intro strictly under 45 seconds when spoken?
- [ ] Does the intro end with a clear bridge/cliffhanger to the deep dive?
- [ ] Are all stage directions/camera cues STRIPPED from the script? (Only spoken words allowed).
- [ ] Is the luggage complete for all platforms?
