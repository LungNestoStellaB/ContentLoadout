# FALCO RESEARCH MISSION: Ben Lamm & Colossal Biosciences
**Label:** 🟡 CONTEXT

**1. Ben Lamm — Background & Credibility**
- **Profile:** 44-year-old billionaire tech entrepreneur ($3.9B estimated net worth as of 2026).
- **Track Record:** Serial entrepreneur with 5+ successful exits before Colossal. He founded and sold Simply Interactive (e-learning), Chaotic Moon Studios (mobile app, to Accenture), Team Chaos (gaming, to Zynga), Conversable (AI bot, to LivePerson), and Hypergiant Industries (AI/Defense, to Trive Capital in 2023).
- **Credibility:** Known for fast execution, securing massive capital, and attracting high-profile talent. Currently focuses on deep tech, synthetic biology, and conservation. Member of the Board of Trustees for The Explorers Club.

**2. Colossal Biosciences — De-Extinction Tech**
- **Mission:** De-extinction of the woolly mammoth, Tasmanian tiger (thylacine), dodo, and recently announced "dire wolf" (April 2025). Uses CRISPR technology on closest living relatives (e.g., Asian elephant for the mammoth).
- **Funding & Valuation:** As of January 2025, raised a **$200M Series C** at an eye-popping **$10.2 Billion valuation**. Total funding exceeds $435M.
- **Investors:** TWG Global (Mark Walter), Thomas Tull, Peter Jackson ($10M for dodo), USIT, Breyer Capital, IQT, Arch Venture Partners, plus celebrities (Paris Hilton, Tom Brady, Chris Hemsworth).

**3. Form Bio — Computational Biology**
- **Status:** Spun out of Colossal in late 2022.
- **Funding:** Raised $30M in early funding (led by Thomas Tull).
- **Focus:** AI-driven computational biology and software platform designed to improve efficiency for scientists in cell/gene therapy and genomics.

**4. Other Ventures in the Ecosystem**
- **Breaking:** Launched in April 2024. A plastic degradation and synthetic biology startup (gestated at Colossal). Discovered X-32, a microbe that breaks down plastics in 22 months. Raised $10.5M seed funding.
- **The Colossal Foundation:** Launched Oct 2024 with $50M. A 501(c)(3) nonprofit focused on biobanking and global conservation efforts.

**5. Investment Angle — Public Exposure**
- **Direct Investment:** Colossal, Form Bio, and Breaking are all **PRIVATE**. No direct public stock exists yet. Series C shares priced at roughly $25/share on private secondary markets like Hiive/EquityZen (accredited only).
- **Adjacent Public Plays (Synthetic Bio / CRISPR):**
  - **CRISPR Therapeutics (CRSP), Intellia Therapeutics (NTLA), Editas Medicine (EDIT), Beam Therapeutics (BEAM):** Public companies holding foundational CRISPR IP, though focused on human medicine rather than de-extinction.
  - **ETFs:** Genomics and Biotech ETFs like ARKG (ARK Genomic Revolution) hold the underlying CRISPR tech platforms that Colossal relies upon.
- **Note:** Do not confuse Colossal with Mammoth Biosciences (a private CRISPR healthcare company).

**6. Timeline for IPO / Investability**
- **Timeline:** Likely years away. Colossal's flagship de-extinction births (mammoth/thylacine) are projected for around **2028**.
- **Revenue Model Pre-IPO:** Colossal plans to spin out tech (like their artificial wombs and Form Bio), secure government conservation contracts, and eventually sell biodiversity credits. They expect these spin-outs and tech licensing to generate revenue long before the animals do.
- **Verdict:** Highly speculative private holding right now. If you want exposure today, you have to buy the underlying "rails" (CRISPR/Gene Editing public companies or ETFs like ARKG) rather than the de-extinction "application layer."
