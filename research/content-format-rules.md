# Content Delivery Format Rules
**Date:** 2026-04-29
**Status:** MANDATORY FOR ALL CONTENT (VI, SA, PW)

## The Strict Format ("The Loadout")
Absolutely no visual cues, no camera directions, and no timestamps. 
The format must contain exactly four elements:
1. **Title:** One clear heading.
2. **Description:** One short descriptive paragraph.
3. **Clean Script:** Only the spoken words, formatted with line breaks for pacing.
4. **Luggage:** The platform-specific metadata (titles, descriptions, tags, and captions for YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram).

## Example (VI 22 Corrected)
Title: The 3-Step AI Voice Scam Defense

Description:
Scammers can clone your voice with just three seconds of audio. Here is a simple, three-step defense plan to protect your family tonight.

Clean Script:
Your phone rings. It's your kid, and they're crying for help.
But what if it's not actually them?
Scammers only need a three-second clip of your voice from social media to clone it perfectly using AI.
It sounds exactly like you. It breathes like you. It panics like you.
It’s terrifying.
But you don't need to be a tech expert to stop it dead in its tracks.
Here is your family's three-step defense. Do this tonight.
Step one. Establish a family safe word right now.
Make it something completely random.
If someone calls asking for money or help, ask for the word.
No word? Hang up.
Step two. If you feel panicked, that's the scammer's goal.
Hang up the phone immediately, and call them right back on the number you have saved in your contacts.
Step three. Stop giving them ammunition.
Lock your social media accounts down to 'friends only' so strangers can't scrape your voice.
This is the easiest way to beat the hackers.
Share this video with your family right now to set up your safe word today.
