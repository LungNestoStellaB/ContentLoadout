# Simulation Directive: $5K Global Swing Trading
Date: 2026-04-12
Origin: Lung Nesto (Captain)
Status: ACTIVE SIMULATION

## Core Portfolio Parameters
- **Starting Capital:** $5,000 USD
- **Position Sizing:** $250 per position (approx. 12-15 positions total)
- **Deployment:** 80-100% invested (spread across uncorrelated global assets)
- **Rollout Phase:** Start conservative with 8-10 positions, scale up to 15+ as consistency is proven.
- **Instruments:** Stocks, ETFs, low-volatility indices/futures (broker must support fractional shares and $0/low commissions).

## Risk Management (Strict)
- **Rule 1:** Risk first, size second, define stop *before* entry.
- **Per-Trade Risk:** 0.5% – 2.0% of total capital ($25 – $100 max loss per trade).
- **Total Portfolio Risk:** Cap at 6% – 8% across all open swings.
- **Diversification:** 2-3 positions max per region/sector to mitigate localized market drops. (e.g., US Tech, Aus Resources, Asia Exporters, EU Indices).

## Trading Strategy & Goals
- **Style:** Low-risk swing trading (holding days to a few weeks).
- **Setup Target:** Clean swings with tight stops and a 1:2+ Reward-to-Risk ratio.
- **Routine:** 30–60 minute evening review to check stops and new setups. Low screen time.
- **Target Yield:** 1% – 2% account growth per month net.
- **Win Rate Target:** 55% – 60% with the 1:2 R:R.

## Psychological Edge
Smaller position sizes ($250) lead to smaller daily P&L swings. This ensures we stay calm, strictly follow the rules, and compound modest wins systematically.
