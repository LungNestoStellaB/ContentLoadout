# Concept: Universal Score Card (USc)
Date: 2026-04-14
Origin: Lung Nesto

## The Premise
To truly gauge our efforts, we need a unified ledger to track the friction and the fuel of the operation. This is the Universal Score Card (USc).

## Metrics to Track
**The Friction (Costs):**
- Subscriptions & Tooling
- API Credits / Intelligence Costs
- Trading Costs (Slippage, commissions)

**The Fuel (Successes/Yield):**
- Content Metrics (Cost per view, engagement yield)
- Portfolio Returns (Net yield vs. friction)

## Implementation Plan
- **Timeline:** Formulate the structure "next week" (Week of April 20th).
- **Owner:** Falco will likely manage the USc, acting as the operational ledger.
- **Goal:** Clarity on "what it took to get to the next step." No hidden operational drag.
