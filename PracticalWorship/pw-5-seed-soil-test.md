# PW — The 5-Seed Soil Test (CLEAN FORMAT)

**Title:** The 5-Seed Soil Test

**Description:** Five seeds from any local market can tell you everything about your soil — no expensive kits, no equipment. Let nature do the testing.

**Script:**
Everyone talks about soil. Dig it, fertilize it, test it with expensive kits. But there are five packets of seeds from any local market that can tell you everything you need to know.

This is the 5-Seed Soil Test. And it costs almost nothing.

First — scatter some mung beans. If they sprout in a few days, your soil is alive and healthy. If they don't — you've got a problem. The beauty — mung beans actually fix nitrogen in the soil, so even if the test fails, the seeds are already helping.

Second — kangkong, morning glory. This one tells you about water. If it thrives, your moisture levels are right. If it struggles, your soil is too dry.

Third — holy basil. This is your pH test. Basil is sensitive. If it grows strong and fragrant, your soil is balanced. If basil is happy, most vegetables will be too.

Fourth — sunflowers. These aren't just beautiful — they're nature's contamination detectors. If they grow tall and healthy, your soil is clean. If they're stunted, you may have heavy metals in the ground.

Fifth — lemongrass. This tests your drainage. If it grows well, water flows through properly. If it rots at the base, your soil is waterlogged. Plus, it keeps the mosquitoes away.

Five seeds. Two weeks. Zero equipment. Let nature do the testing for you.

This is Practical Worship. Honouring the land by learning from it.
