# PW EPISODE 8 — "Grow What You Actually Eat"
*(Stella 2.0 — filed by Stella 4.0)*

**Theme:** Don't grow what looks impressive. Grow what leaves your kitchen fastest.

---

## ENGLISH CLEAN SCRIPT

Most people start a garden by asking,
"What grows well here?"

That's not the first question.

The first question is:
What do you cook every week?

If you buy chillies every few days — grow chillies.
If you use basil every week — grow basil.
If you cook with lemongrass — plant it once and keep cutting.

Don't grow what looks impressive.
Grow what leaves your kitchen fastest.

One pot of spring onions can save you dozens of trips.
One kaffir lime tree can flavour a hundred meals.
A few herbs near the door can change the way you cook.

A useful garden beats a beautiful garden that nobody touches.

Start with your plate.
Then plant what your life already needs.

Practical Worship.
