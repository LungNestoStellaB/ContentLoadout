# PRACTICAL WORSHIP (PW): STRATEGIC BLUEPRINT
**Theme:** King Bhumibol's Sufficiency Economy Philosophy
**Core Focus:** Personal sustainability, growing food/herbs, the science of personal agriculture. Living simply, living well.
**Risk Management:** Renamed from "For the King" to "Practical Worship" to entirely bypass Thai lèse-majesté laws. The frame: The action (sustainability) is the worship.

## 1. THE PRODUCTION ARCHITECTURE (Multi-Language Scale)
PW is built from Day 1 for global distribution. It does not exist in one language.
*   **Format:** Separate YouTube channels per language.
*   **Current Rollout:**
    *   Episode 1: English, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Chinese
    *   Episode 2: Vietnamese, Indonesian, English
    *   Thai Version: Utilizes a specific Thai presenter avatar.
*   **Visual Engine:** Sora-generated backgrounds combined with Synthesia/HeyGen avatars and automated language dubbing.

## 2. THE DISTRIBUTION & IMPACT
PW is not just passive content; it is connected to on-the-ground reality via the **Helping Hand volunteer group**. It bridges digital philosophy with physical sustainability practices.
